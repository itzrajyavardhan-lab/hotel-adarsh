Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?
Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 Noon. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability. Premium and Royal Suite guests enjoy flexible timings.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
Breakfast is complimentary for Royal Suite and Family Suite bookings. For Deluxe and Premium rooms, breakfast can be added for ₹450/person. Our breakfast spread is available from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled check-in. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to one night's charge. No-shows will be charged the full stay amount.
Do you offer airport transfers?
Yes! We offer complimentary airport transfers for Royal Suite guests. For other room categories, transfers are available at ₹1,200 one-way. Please inform us at least 2 hours before arrival for arrangements.
Are pets allowed at Hotel Adarsh?
We welcome well-behaved pets in select rooms with prior notice. A pet fee of ₹1,000/night applies. Please contact us before booking to confirm pet-friendly room availability.
Can I host events or private functions?
Absolutely! We have elegant banquet halls and garden spaces for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations. Our event planning team can customise packages for groups of 20 to 500 guests. Contact us for bespoke event proposals.
